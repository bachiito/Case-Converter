Case Converter

Upper Case

The upper case will take any text and convert all of the letters to capital letters, it is the case when each letter is in the upper case. It will convert all lower case letters to Capital letters.

For example, THIS IS THE UPPER CASE TEXT.

Lower Case

The lower case allows you to uncapitalize your text, it is the case when each letter is in the lower case. It converts all of the letters in your text to lowercase.

For example, this is the lower case text.

Proper Case

The proper case is the case when each word starts with the upper case, and the rest of the word is in the lower. It will automatically change the first letter of each word to upper case and leave the remaining letters in lower case.

For example, This Is The Proper Case Text.

Sentence Case

The sentence case is the case when each sentence start swith an upper case letter, and the rest of the sentence is in the lower case. It works by capitalizing the first letter of every sentence before transforming the rest of the text to lowercase.

For example, This is the sentence case. Only the first word of the sentence starts with an upper case letter.
